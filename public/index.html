About Us

BLAC Financial is a small firm that has been serving the GTA since 1991. We are committed to providing our clients (individuals & businesses) with innovative support, knowledge and advice. We have extensive experience in business, personal and estate tax returns. We also specialize in GST/HST Tax filing.

Our professional consultants aim to take the stress out of tax season by simplifying that process for our clients. We ensure that all your deductions are utilized to minimize your tax payable. Whether your tax information is current or in arrears we can help! Call today with any tax return requirements. Late evening appointments and Drop-Off Service available.

We are not just your neighborhood tax office, we also provide other services that business owners may need. We understand small business because we are one.
