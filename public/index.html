About Us

BLAC is a small business firm that has been serving the GTA since 1991 and is committed to providing all our clients (individuals & businesses) with the best support, knowledge and advice. We have extensive experience in business, personal and estate tax returns. We also specialize in GST/HST Tax filing.

Our professional consultants aim to take the stress out of tax season by simplifying that process for our clients. They ensure that all your deductions are utilised so that you pay the minimum tax allowable. Whether your tax information is current or in arrears they can help! Call today for any tax return requirements. Late evening appointments and Drop-Off Service available.

We are not just your neighborhood tax office, we also provide other services that business owners may need. See our Services Page for more information on all the services we provide.
